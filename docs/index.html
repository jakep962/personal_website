About myself

I am a theoretical biologist currently working in the Hurford Population+ lab at Memorial University in St. John’s Newfoundland. I build mathematical and statistical models to model population dynamics and make long-term predictions for salmon lice Lepeophtheirus salmonis. My research experience is focused on the impact of environmental conditions, but I have broad interests ranging from population dynamics, ecosystem modeling and epidemiology to medical applications.
